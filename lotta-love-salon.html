Liner Notes

The story behind the name

Lotta Love Salon & Cutz opened on Mattis Avenue with one idea: everybody deserves to leave the chair feeling looked after. Half barbershop, half salon, all family — we cut, highlight, lock, and press for every kind of hair walking through that door, biracial hair included.

The owner's fabulous highlights and cuts keep clients loyal, and the shop stays clean, welcoming, and full of good conversation. Walk-ins are welcome, but a call ahead means less waiting and more chair time.

Why "Side A / Side B"?

Every great record has two sides. Side A is the cutz — sharp, quick, straight to the point. Side B is the salon — longer sits, deeper transformations. Different tempo, same care.

New here? Ask whichever stylist answers the phone what they'd recommend for your hair type — that's basically our whole business model.
